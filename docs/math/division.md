|名前|効果・戻り値|
|:--|:--|
|`floor_div(a, b)`|$\left\lfloor \frac{A}{B} \right\rfloor$|
|`ceil_div(a, b)`|$\left\lceil \frac{A}{B} \right\rceil$|
